Privacy Policy

ShiftTrack — Shift Schedule Tracker

Effective date: 23 March 2026

ShiftTrack ("the app") is developed by Sergei Likharev ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data we collect

When you sign in with your Google account, we receive and store:

  • Account information — your display name, email address, and a unique user identifier provided by Google.

When you use the app, you create and store:

  • Shift schedule data — your anchor date, cycle index, and any manual shift overrides.
  • Leave records — leave dates, types (annual, sick, personal, unpaid, other), half-day status, and notes.
  • Overtime records — overtime dates, hours, and notes.
  • Leave balances — annual leave allocations and used days per category.
  • App preferences — display settings, widget configuration, colour preferences, and spectator mode status.
  • Invite links — viewer invite tokens you generate to share your schedule.

2. How we use your data

  • To display your personalised shift schedule, leave records, and overtime information.
  • To sync your data across devices via cloud storage so you do not lose it.
  • To send push notifications related to your schedule (e.g. shift reminders), if you opt in.
  • To allow viewers you invite to see your schedule in spectator mode.

3. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — in a local database and encrypted preferences.
  • In the cloud — in Google Firebase (Firestore) servers located in the EU/US, protected by Firebase's security rules and encryption in transit and at rest.

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication via Google Sign-In. We do not store your Google password.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

Your data is shared only with:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, cloud storage, and push notifications. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Viewers you invite — if you generate an invite link, the recipient can view your shift schedule in read-only mode.

5. Analytics and advertising

The app does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics or serve advertisements.

6. Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Access your data — all your data is visible within the app.
  • Export your data — your schedule, leave, and overtime records are accessible directly in the app.
  • Delete your data — tap "Delete my account and all data" in the app's Settings screen. This permanently removes all your data from both the device and cloud storage. See our account deletion page for details.
  • Revoke Google access — you can revoke ShiftTrack's access to your Google account at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

8. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all data is permanently erased immediately — both locally and from cloud storage. No data is retained after deletion.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at: ikusurei@gmail.com
