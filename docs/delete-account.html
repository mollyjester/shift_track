Delete Your Account

ShiftTrack — Shift Schedule Tracker

How to delete your account

  1. Open the ShiftTrack app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete my account and all data".
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials
  • All shift schedule data (overrides and settings)
  • All leave records (annual, sick, personal, unpaid, other)
  • Leave balance history
  • All overtime records and balances
  • App preferences and widget configuration
  • Any viewer invite links you have generated

Retention & timing

Account deletion is processed immediately. All data stored in our cloud database (Google Firestore) and your local app storage is permanently erased. No data is retained after deletion.

Note: If you are unable to access the app, please contact us using the email below and we will process the deletion on your behalf within 3 business days.
